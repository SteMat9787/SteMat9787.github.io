4 We then tested within-category decoding: Can the brain tell apart individual images within a category?

6 In addition, we ran a control experiment where typical participants viewed altered images to mimic the visual deficits of the cataract group.

8 In sum:

9 Together, these results reveal different sensitive periods across the visual system:

11 The message?
